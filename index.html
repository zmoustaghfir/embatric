About EMBATRIC

Founded in the year 2021, our club emerged as a passionate initiative by the students of Embedded Systems and Electrical Engineering at FST Beni Mellal. At its core, our club is a testament to the dedication, ingenuity, and collaborative spirit of our members. With a shared vision of advancing the frontiers of electronics and technology, we embarked on this journey with one clear mission: to create a dynamic platform where knowledge, innovation, and camaraderie flourish.
